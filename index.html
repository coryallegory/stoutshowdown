Info

Wikipedia does a nice job of going over a bit of the history and what constitutes a Stout, Porter, Dry/Irish Stout, Milk Stout- needless to say there is a lot of overlap.

Beer Eligibility

We are only concerned with beers meeting the following criteria:
  • Specifically called out as a "Stout"
  • Available at Manitoba Liquor Marts (if a beer is discontinued it might be pulled from the running)
  • Available in tallboy (473mL) cans, this may be relaxed as we go...

Showdown Format

Bracketed tournament style, three at a time, with a tasting deadline. For each matchup you'll taste and assign 3, 2, 1 points to each beer based on which you think is the best. The beer with the most points in each matchup moves onto the next round, until a favorite is crowned.

How To Participate

Anyone can join in at any time, provided you can get the relevant beers. Ideally we'd meet up in person to split cans and have some fun, but given the current times this will need to be done remotely.

Acquire and taste the beers at any time, then message Cory your rankings for each set of matchups. Each matchup has a deadline for counting votes.
