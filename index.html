Spring Stout Showdown

Wikipedia does a nice job of going over a bit of the history and what constitutes a Stout, Porter, Dry/Irish Stout, Milk Stout- needless to say there is a lot of overlap.

We are only concerned with beers meeting the following criteria:
- Specifically called out as a "Stout"
- Available at Manitoba Liquor Marts
- Available in tallboy cans

There are a good number of them, this will roll out in a bracketed tournament style comparison, three at a time. The winners of each matchup will progress until a favorite is crowned.

Ideally a can would be split 3-4 ways for a taste, but we'll all have to take one for the team remotely given the times.

Each round you will assign 3, 2, 1 points to each beer in order of your preference based on whatever criteria you would like to use personally. The beer with the most points overall moves onto the next round.
