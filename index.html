Info

Wikipedia does a nice job of going over a bit of the history and what constitutes a Stout, Porter, Dry/Irish Stout, Milk Stout- needless to say there is a lot of overlap.
Beer Eligibility
We are only concerned with beers meeting the following criteria:
  • Specifically called out as a "Stout"
  • Available at Manitoba Liquor Marts in Manitoba (if a beer is discontinued it might be pulled from the running)
  • Available in cans. tallboy (473mL) cans, this may be relaxed as we go...
Showdown Format
Bracketed tournament style, three at a time. For each matchup you'll taste and assign 3, 2, 1 points to each beer based on which you think is the best. The beer with the most points in each matchup moves onto the next round, until a favorite is ultimately crowned. Ties are broken based on the beer with the most first, second third place votes comparatively.
How To Participate
Anyone can join in at any time, provided you can get the relevant beers. Ideally we'd meet up in person to split cans and have some fun, but given current circumstances this will need to be done remotely.

Acquire and taste the beers at any time, then message Cory your rankings for each set of matchups. Each matchup has a deadline for your votes to count and keep this moving.

*The MLCC website is pretty good at tracking "store inventory", you can click the Links icon in a given row in the Beer List to help track down some of the trickier ones.
